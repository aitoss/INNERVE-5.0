Themes

Sometimes! Creative impulses played out, you know!.. So themes are not fixed this time. Below themes are just to give you a generalized idea..

Health care


Looking at Healthcare in a completely new way - focusing on earlier, pre-symptomatic disease detection and prevention, instead of late diagnosis - helping clinicians access more information and intervene sooner with targeted treatments so their patients can lead longer, fuller lives.

Covid


It's an open opportunity for all the developers out there to build software solutions that drive social impact, aim to tackle some of the challenges related to the current coronavirus (COVID-19) pandemic.

Transportation


The pandemic has affected the transportation system of India. It is our duty to help India restore the system and make it safe and reliable for the citizen of India. It is the need of the hour to focus on the increasing problems due to the transportation system. It is the responsibility of the developers to discover ideas, business models, and prototypes to evolve the technology.

Smart City


Ever got the chance of making your city a better place? Well, now is the time to think and act. Gather information and together, in a collaborative fashion, build solutions that will make our city a more efficient and intelligent place to live.

5-G Tech


Next-generation 5G networks will boost, automate and enable faster communication between devices and servers leading to smart farming. Examples- smart drones equipped with multispectral imaging, sensors monitoring the health of crops, hands-free autonomous tractors, smart soil analysis, AR bases animal care, connected livestock.

EduTech


Technology has been considered central to the reform of school education and has gained unprecedented momentum during this pandemic. When the pandemic had resulted in schools shut all across the world, over 1.2 billion children were out of the classroom. Online teaching was perceived as the only immediate means to combat all the education-related issues, hence there was a rush to transfer classrooms into the virtual world without taking into consideration the reach to all the learners.
